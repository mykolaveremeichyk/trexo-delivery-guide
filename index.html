01

Overview

This document describes the complete process of importing a Trexo Robotics device from Canada to Poland, based on the actual experience of a November 2024 shipment. It covers all parties involved, required documents, payment steps, customs clearance, and final delivery.

The entire process from initial invoice to final delivery took approximately 2–3 weeks. The goods travel by air from Pearson International Airport (Toronto) to Warsaw Chopin Airport, where they are cleared by a Polish customs broker before last-mile delivery.

03

Step-by-Step Process

1

Receive freight invoice from Polimex

Polimex (Helen) will email the freight invoice and their bank details for payment. Review the invoice carefully before paying.

2

Confirm consignee name & delivery address

Clarify with Polimex whose name goes on the Air Waybill (AWB). The consignee can be the child's name if the device is for a child. Provide the full Poland delivery address.

3

Pay the freight invoice

Transfer the invoice amount to Polimex's bank account. After payment, send a scan/screenshot of the confirmation to Helen by email.

4

Trexo palletizes the goods

Trexo may palletize boxes into a single pallet — this can significantly increase the chargeable weight. In this case weight increased from 126 kg to 243 kg, requiring an additional payment of USD 327.50. Always expect this possibility.

5

Pay any additional weight surcharge

If a weight adjustment invoice is issued, pay it to the same Polimex bank account and send payment confirmation. Polimex will not release the shipment until all payments are confirmed.

6

Provide AWB + invoices to customs broker (ZCL)

Ask Polimex for the HAWB copy and Trexo commercial invoices. Forward these to ZCL. Note: Trexo may not provide a separate packing list — use the commercial invoices instead.

7

Sign Power of Attorney (POA) for customs broker

ZCL needs a POA authorizing them to handle customs clearance on your behalf. Set this up in advance to avoid airport storage charges.

8

Shipment departs Canada

Polimex ships the goods by air freight. Alfa Forwarding in Poland will receive a pre-alert with commercial documents and will revert with a DSK number when ready.

9

Goods arrive at Warsaw Chopin Airport (WAW)

Alfa Forwarding (Żaneta) notifies you of arrival and sends a POD and Notification of Arrival. Note: storage fees begin 2 days after arrival.

10

Customs clearance begins

ZCL prepares and submits the customs import declaration electronically. You do NOT need to visit the customs office in person.

11

Customs clearance completed

ZCL sends you an MRN number and a customs clearance document. Forward this immediately to Alfa Forwarding to authorize the shipment release.

12

Local delivery arranged by Alfa Forwarding

Żaneta will send you the driver's details and expected arrival time. Be ready to contact the driver directly on delivery day.

13

Receive the delivery

Meet the driver at your delivery address. Call the driver around the indicated time. Confirm receipt by email to Alfa Forwarding.

14

Receive insurance certificate

After delivery, Polimex will send a cargo insurance certificate for your records.

Air Freight Transit Time

Approximately 3 days from pickup in Canada to arrival at Warsaw Chopin Airport. The flight in this case departed 15-Nov-24 and arrived 17-Nov-24. Allow additional time for customs clearance (typically 1–2 business days) and last-mile delivery (same day or next day after clearance).

ℹ️ The Polimex invoice does not include Polish customs clearance fees. Those are charged separately by the Polish customs broker. Request a separate quote from ZCL or your chosen broker.

07

Key Tips & Lessons Learned

Before Shipment

💡Set up the Power of Attorney (POA) for your customs broker before the goods arrive at the airport. Delays cost storage fees.
💡Expect Trexo to palletize boxes — the chargeable weight may significantly increase due to pallet dimensions.
💡Confirm the consignee name early. If the device is for a child, the child's name may be more appropriate for customs purposes.
💡Ask Polimex for the HAWB and Trexo invoices as soon as available, and send them immediately to ZCL.

During Customs Clearance

💡You do NOT need to visit the customs office. ZCL submits the import declaration electronically.
💡ZCL will ask for documents — respond quickly to avoid airport storage accumulating.
💡Storage starts shortly after arrival (2 days in this case). Act fast after the arrival notification.
💡Monitor for the customs clearance certificate from ZCL — forward it immediately to Alfa Forwarding when received.

Day of Delivery

💡Stay available by phone on the scheduled delivery day.
💡You will receive the driver's name, vehicle plate, and phone number from Alfa Forwarding.
💡Confirm delivery receipt to Alfa Forwarding by email after pickup.

Glossary

AWB / HAWB
Air Waybill / House Air Waybill — the key transport document for air freight, identifying the shipment
POD
Proof of Delivery — document confirming the shipment arrived at the airport warehouse
DSK
Airport warehouse reference number used for releasing the shipment to the customs broker
POA
Power of Attorney — document authorizing ZCL to act on your behalf for customs clearance
MRN
Movement Reference Number — official customs clearance reference issued by Polish customs
SAD
Single Administrative Document — the customs import declaration filed by ZCL
Chargeable Weight
The weight used for billing — can be actual weight or volumetric weight (whichever is higher). Palletizing can increase this significantly.
WAW
Warsaw Chopin International Airport — where the shipment arrived in Poland
EORI
Economic Operators Registration and Identification — required for customs in the EU
GST
Canadian Goods and Services Tax — zero-rated on international exports, so no tax applied to this shipment
